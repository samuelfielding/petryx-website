Want to do geoscience faster?

Do you want to speed up your projects, reduce errors & cut costs? Are you still manually working with your geoscience data in spreadsheets, text files, pdfs & word documents? If so, Petryx can help.

Petryx are a consultancy specialised in automating how geoscientists gather, process, analyse, visualise & report data. We build bespoke on-premise or cloud-hosted automations using R & Python to help you work faster with data.

Automated data cleaning & manipulation

Minimise human error & time spent by automating how you collate, copy & change data. Speed up moving data between different third-party software.

Automate how you check data for errors, and how you flag & fix those errors.

Automated analysis & visualisation

Analyse your data & visualise it with custom charts and maps at the click of a button.

Automation means charts can be completely standardised across your projects & cutting edge plotting packages mean ultra-high resolution images.

Automated report creation

Automatically generate reports from data using custom templates & visualisations. Boilerplate text sections can be quickly populated with values, figures & results.

And reports can quickly be regenerated if you only spot an error late in the process. No more frantic, last-minute scrambles to make corrections.

Create reports in pdf, docx or as an interactive webpage you can share with stakeholders.
