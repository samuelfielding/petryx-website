Reducing reservoir quality risk

Petryx wanted to come up with an efficient way of lowering the total cost of recovery and reducing cycle times for hydrocarbon explorers by allowing them to screen reservoir quality potential in regions or blocks where seismic or well data were not yet available.

We developed a unique method for making quantitative predictions of clastic reservoir quality potential both laterally and stratigraphically in data-poor areas.

Combining the power of predictive computation, massive globally distributed datasets and sediment process expertise, we provide a cost-efficient solution for better preparing explorers before committing capital to new ventures.

Predictive computation

We've distilled the most up-to-date scientific research and the classics to build predictive models for quantifying the sediment compositional characteristics which influence reservoir quality on block and basin scales.

Our algorithms undergo continuous improvement and are ground-truthed using reservoir data from different modern and past sedimentary systems.

Massive, globally distributed data

To feed our predictive capability we've spent almost three years aggregating and standardising massive amounts of raw geological and geochemical data.

Now, with over 140 million data points, we can apply our models in almost all of the world's sedimentary basins.
